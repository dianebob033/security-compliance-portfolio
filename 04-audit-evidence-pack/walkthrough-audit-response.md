## Scenario
Auditor requests evidence for logging, access control, and encryption controls.

## Steps
1. Identify requested controls (Control IDs)
2. Pull evidence per Evidence Index
3. Validate timestamps and access restrictions
4. Provide evidence package + explanation notes
5. Track follow-ups and remediation items
